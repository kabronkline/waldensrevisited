Our Story

Inspired by Walden

Like Thoreau's retreat to Walden Pond, our community embraces the idea that the richest life is one lived close to nature.

Walden's Revisited is a small, covenant-protected neighborhood located in Scioto Township, Delaware County, Ohio. Our community is built around a central pond and shared common areas that connect neighbors to the land and to each other.

Situated along Brindle Road (Township Road 170) near Slocum Road, our tracts of land offer the space, quiet, and natural beauty that define rural Ohio living—while being just a short drive from the amenities of Delaware and Columbus.

Life Here

Shared Spaces

Common Areas

Our community includes approximately 31 acres of common use easement areas shared among the residents. These areas encompass the central pond, surrounding meadows, and natural corridors that connect the individual tracts.

The common areas are maintained collectively by the property owners and are subject to the community's protective covenants and restrictions.

Guidelines

Covenants & Restrictions

Our community is guided by the Restated Declaration of Protective Covenants and Restrictions for Walden's Revisited, filed in 2019 with Delaware County.

These covenants exist to preserve the rural character, natural beauty, and peaceful atmosphere that make our community special. They cover topics including:

Land Use

Guidelines for residential use, agriculture, and maintaining the rural character of the community.

Building Standards

Requirements for structures to ensure they complement the natural landscape and maintain property values.

Common Areas

Rules for the shared use and maintenance of common easement areas, including the pond and surrounding land.

Environmental Stewardship

Provisions to protect the water quality of the pond, preserve native vegetation, and support local wildlife.

For the complete text of the covenants, property owners may reference the filed documents at the Delaware County Recorder's Office (Book 1372, Pages 1033-1064 for the original; restated version filed 2019).
